# Controller Guide

## Purpose And Scope

This guide is for controllers and accounting staff responsible for actuals, reconciliations, close, consolidation, statutory support, and audit evidence. Controller users usually need access to ledger depth, close and reconciliation, consolidation, reporting, evidence, and audit packet workflows.

## Controller Workspace

Use the controller workspace to monitor:

- Actuals, budget, forecast, and scenario separation.
- Journal adjustment workflow.
- Account reconciliations and aging.
- Close task dependencies.
- Period lock state.
- Consolidation runs, eliminations, entity confirmations, and audit packets.

Start each close cycle by confirming the active fiscal period and entity hierarchy.

## Review Cadence

During close week, review open tasks daily. During normal operations, review exceptions at least weekly:

1. New journal adjustments pending review.
2. Reconciliations waiting for preparer or reviewer action.
3. Aging exceptions and unmatched intercompany balances.
4. Failed imports or rejected actuals.
5. Open audit evidence requests.

## Reconciliations

Each reconciliation should have a preparer, reviewer, aging status, variance explanation, and evidence when required. The preparer should not approve their own reconciliation unless a documented emergency override exists and SoD policy allows it.

Use aging and exception views to focus review time on unresolved differences. Attach supporting schedules, bank statements, grant documentation, or ERP extracts as evidence.

## Consolidation

Before running consolidation:

1. Confirm entity hierarchy and ownership percentages.
2. Confirm currency and GAAP placeholders are configured for the current reporting basis.
3. Resolve intercompany matches.
4. Submit eliminations for review.
5. Confirm period lock rules are enforced.

After consolidation, review consolidation audit reports, currency translation adjustments, minority interest, and supplemental schedules before releasing reports.

## Signoff

Close signoff requires:

- All required tasks complete.
- Reconciliations prepared and reviewed.
- Exceptions either cleared or documented.
- Entity confirmations complete.
- Consolidation run reviewed.
- Audit packet assembled with evidence links.

Lock the period only after signoff. If a locked period must be reopened, document the reason, approver, and expected reposting impact.

## Evidence Retention

Use comments and attachments for close tasks, reconciliations, reports, and audit packets. Evidence should identify source system, reporting period, preparer, reviewer, and date. Do not store confidential source files outside approved campus storage unless the data owner approves it.
